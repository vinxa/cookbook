# Char_Kway_Teow_Beef

## Ingredients

- *300 grams* lean beef fillet
- *1.5 tbsp* soy sauce
- *2 tsp* chinese wine
- *1.5 tbsp* oyster sauce
- *1 tsp* white sugar
- *1 tsp* cornflour
- *1 tsp* sesame oil
- *1 tbsp* canola oil
- *1.5 tbsp* canola oil
- *500 grams* bean sprouts
- *1 tbsp* canola oil
- *5* minced garlic cloves
- *600 grams* rice noodles or kway teow
- *3.5 tbsp* canola oil
- *4* sliced ginger

## Cookware

- wok
- wok
- wok

## Steps

1. Slice lean beef fillet thinly against the grain.

2. Mix beef with seasoning ingredients: soy sauce, chinese wine, oyster sauce,
white sugar, and cornflour. Leave to marinate fro 20min, then add sesame oil and
canola oil.

3. Heat canola oil in wok. Stir fry bean sprouts until slightly limp, then set
aside.

4. Heat canola oil in wok. Saute minced garlic cloves until crispy and golden.
Add rice noodles or kway teow and stir fry for 4 minutes until lightly browned.
Set aside.

5. Heat canola oil in wok. Add beef and remaining marinade and sliced ginger.

6. Add noodles and bean sprouts, stir fry until everything is well coated.
